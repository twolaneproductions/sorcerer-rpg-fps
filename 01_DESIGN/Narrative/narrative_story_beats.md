# Story

## Prologue - Cutscene

* Very simple cutscene -- what the world is like over some early indications of the world.
* Why the heroic protagonist is needed.

## Part One

* Start approaching the gates of the TOWN. Still out in the woods, we come across *a couple* in trouble with *some demons*.
* We intervene (combat tutorial) to save them.
* With the demons defeated, we escort the couple to the gates of town. They provide us with lore and point us in the direction of town and the Castle.
* Passing through town, there's not much to do until we approach the Castle. The *guards* will not admit us, disbelieving that we are the sorcerer's apprentice until we *zap* one of them with a little magic and are granted access.
* Once inside the Castle, we are granted access to *the royal family* and speak to the *King*.
    * He informs us that *our master* agreed to aid the kingdom by heading North to confront *the Banished Lord* but has not returned.
    * The King extends the same generous offer of rewards to us that he did to our master if we can return with proof that the Banished has been slain.
* In the town, at the *Chapel*, where we rest/restore our health, we encounter *the priest*.
    * He discusses the *Castle Ruins* and relate a story about their former inhabitant:
    * There was *an old wizard* who previously dwelt nearby in town, but because of his noisy experiments, one day he was driven off by the frightened townsfolk.
    * The priest was a friend of the wizard's and was sad to see him chased away. Inspecting the remains of the wizard's former abode, the priest discovered some strange *Artifact* left behind and took it into his possession for safekeeping, in hope to one day return it to the wizard.
    * The priest said that he last heard rumors that the wizard had taken up residence in the Castle Ruins to the north. Since we are venturing that way, he *gives us the Artifact*.
* After *buying equipment* in town, we head out through the wilderness.

## Part Two
 
* In *the woods*, we encounter *goblins* and *demonic wolves*.
* Arriving at the Castle Ruins, we find it inaccessible.
* A nearby *pool* houses the *Wraith of the Wizard* who dwelt in the Castle Ruins but was slain by the Banished Lord. We see his reflection there, whether we have the Artifact yet or not.
* With the Artifact, the Wizard Wraith will be summoned.
    * In exchange for the Artifact, the Wizard offers to show the player a way into the Castle Ruins.
    * He drags them beneath the water and deposits them in **the Moat**.

## Part Three

* The player appears teleported into the murky waters of the Moat. He is disoriented and not sure in which direction the surface lies (it's not up, but the bubbles provide a clue) but must swim to it before he runs out of air.
* After emerging onto the embankment, the Sorcerer must make his way into the castle. We will encounter we encounter *skeletons*, *zombies*, and *various other monsters*.
    * We must cross the **drawbridge** and battle past a couple of *sentry ghouls*.
    * In the **barbican**, we encounter...
    * Inside the **courtyard**, there are a handful of **soldiers** and **peasants zombies** still hanging around the fires.
    * Inside the **hall**, he must pass through a small, rotten feast, dispatching those present.  
    * In the **chambers**, there are a **demonic prince and princess** who put up a mighty fight.
    * After ridding the world of them, the Sorcerer must not overlook the **hatchway** leading down to **the dungeons**.

## Part Four

* Inside **the Dungeon**, the airs streak with the lights of deeper magical fires, and the echoes of Latin can be heard growing as we travel deeper and deeper.
* We must fight **a small horde of demonic knights** to advance.
* Breaking into **the deepest cell** of the dungeon, we face **our master**, who is cooperating with a **demonic warlock** to summon a **portal** before the **Banished Lord**.  
* Once we defeat the two wizards trying to open the portal, before we can use our own magic to close it, the Banished Lord *beheads us*.
* *From the view of our severed head on the dungeon floor*, we watch the portal finally snap open.
* But we are not dead and can still mutter incantations, bringing our body back to life. We can still control it and must use it to *defeat the Banished Lord*.
* Then, at last, we must *seal the Portal*, just as the walls of the Castle come crumbling down over us, burying it all.
