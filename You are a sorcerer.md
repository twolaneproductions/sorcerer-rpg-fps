You are a sorcerer, venturing into the belly of your enemy’s castle in a demonic realm, where they are torturing your estranged master, attempting to possess his soul. You climb up out of the moat, through the gate, past the various wards, into the dungeon, where your enemies await. You must do battle with them. After freeing your master, a demon knight severs your head, compelling your master to channel the last bits of his soul into you, granting you the power to destroy the knight and the pillars of the castle, burying you all beneath the rubble.

# Gameplay

* Magic “ammunition” where each different “ether” or “elixir” refills the “ammunition” for different magic spells  
  * ethers  
  * magic water, sage's elixir, or elfin elixir  
    * phoenix elixir for fire spells  
    * elixir of Thor for electicity

# Story

* The player, **SORCERER**, appears, teleported, into the murky waters of a **moat**. He is disoriented and not sure in which direction the surface lies (it's not up) but must swim to it before he runs out of air.  
* After emerging onto the embankment, Sorcerer must cross the **drawbridge** and battle past a couple of **sentries** in the barbican before passing through the gate to enter the courtyard.  
* Inside the **courtyard**, there are a handful of **soldiers** and **demonic peasants** still hanging around the fires, whom Sorcerer must kill.  
* Inside the **hall**, he must pass through a small feast, dispatching those present.  
* In the **chambers**, there are a **demonic prince and princess** who put up a fight against the sorcerer. After ridding the world of them, the Sorcerer must not overlook the **hatchway** leading down to the dungeons.  
* Inside the **dungeon**, the airs streak with the lights of deeper magical fires and the echoes of Latin can be heard growing as Sorcerer travels deeper and deeper. He must fight **a small horde of demonic knights** outside of the door to the deepest cell.  
* Breaking into the depths of the dungeon, Sorcerer faces **his master**, another human sorcerer, forming a **portal** before a demonic warlock, with the **Demonic Knight Lord** standing beside.  
  * As Sorcerer tries to interrupt the formation of the portal, the Lord beheads him, causing the player to fall lifelessly to the floor. But as the portal finally snaps open, the severed head on the floor begins muttering incantations, bringing the body back to life, though it first begins spewing out blood, then magical sparks, as Sorcerer’s body fires of spells **destroying the Lord, the Warlock, the Master, and finally the Portal and the foundations of the castle**, burying all the demonic corpses and himself.
