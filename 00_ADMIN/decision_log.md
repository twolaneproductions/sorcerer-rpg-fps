# Decision Log

This document serves as a running log of key decisions made during the development of the Sorcerer RPG FPS project. It records the decision, alternatives considered, rationale, and the person responsible for the call to ensure transparency and accountability.

| Date | Decision Made | Alternatives Considered | Rationale | Who Made the Call |
|------------|--------------------------------|------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------|
| 2026-04-22 | Select UZDoom as the game engine | Unity, Unreal Engine, Godot | Familiarity with Doom modding community, open-source nature, and suitability for FPS mechanics. | Lead Developer |
| 2026-04-22 | Target PC platform only | Include console ports, mobile adaptation | Focus on PC audience interested in Doom-style games, limited resources for multi-platform development. | Project Manager |
| 2026-04-27 | Reduce scope to one-hour runtime | Develop full-length game, episodic release | Small indie team constraints, aim for polished short experience to build audience. | Lead Designer |
